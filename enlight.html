‘Enlightenment’ – A workshop on exploring the self through Meditation.

Spiritual Research Cell is a novel initiative to uplift the virtues and to build up the moral muscle of the ‘to-be’ engineers. It is the perfect place to nurture the inner-self with power of meditation.

‘Enlightenment’ is a two hour workshop specially designed for young explorers who embark on journey of self-discovery. This journey will create path ways for you into a future that is rich with happiness and success.

In this exciting 2-hr workshop you will get a chance to:
1. Clarify your understanding on Spirituality.
2. A chance for psychological testing.
3. Experience live Meditation Session.
4. Rejuvenate through creative activity.
5. Special exhibition on Science & Spirituality.
6. Visit of Spiritual Research Cell.

1. Individual entry.
2. Limited 30 seats per day. Registration on first come first serve basis.
3. Participants must carry passes during the event.
4. Participants must reach 15 mins before the commencement of workshop.
