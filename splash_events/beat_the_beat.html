Beat The Beats

Music gives soul to the universe,wings to mind,flight to
imagination,and life to everything.The character of instrumental
music let the emotion radiate and shine in their own character.

Round I: In round one, participants will be given time for 210 seconds (3.5 min.) each to play any instrument
in which they have excelled their skills and enthrall the audience and judges with their melodious performance.

Round II: The participants selected in the 1st round will move ahead and will get a chance to make their 2nd
appearance at the stage. In this round the participants will be given 300 seconds (5 min.) and they have to
match their tone on the instruments with a track playing in the background.

Note : Participants needs to carry their own tracks in pen drives for Round 2.

Entry fees – Rs. 200/- per participant
1st prize – Rs 2000/-
2nd prize – Rs 1000/-
