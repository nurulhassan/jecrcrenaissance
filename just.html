We invite you to participate in the largest technical event of Renaissance 2018, featuring cash prizes worth over Rs. 30000. The event consists of two rounds a Quiz as well as a Competitive Programming round which will test your understanding of the C programming language, Computer Science fundamentals as well as your ability to creatively put it all together to write programs which solve problems inspired by real life situations.

1. Contestants can be from any college and stream and must participate as individuals.
2. No outside references will be allowed.
3. Round 1 will be an hour long MCQ quiz with qualifiers progressing to Round 2.
4. Round 2 will be a competitive programming round lasting for two and a half hours-- contestants will write programs which solve the problems under given time and memory constraints.
5. All decisions taken by the organizing committee will be final and binding.
