    LET ME SPEAK

    Each participant will be given maximum of three minutes to speak extempore on a topic picked up by him or her in both the rounds . A bunch of the topics will be given to select any one randomly with the help of daat . He/she will be given one minute prior to the speech to think over the topic.

  • Maximum time limit for thinking over the topic will be 1 minute and maximum time for speech will be 3 minutes
  • The participant will be allowed to select the topic maximum twice. In case he or she decides to select the topic second time, then he or she will not be allowed to take up the first topic
  • Participant will not be allowed to take any paper or material with him or her during the speech.
