    TECHNOPHILIA

    This event is to develop research aptitude among engineering students. Future of education belongs to research. Students who want to pursue higher studies in India or abroad are very much inquisitive about developing research aptitude . Technophilia will provide them a solid platform to begin.Event also aims to refine verbal and non-verbal communication skills of it’s participants.

  • The event consists of 2 rounds.
  • Students from anybranch can take part in the given event.
  • Presentations should be in .ppt or .pptx format.
  • For the presentations, a LCD projector, a computerwill be provided.
  • The time for one presentation will be 5-7 minutes. One buzzer will be rung after 5 minutes to remind the participants about the time and final buzzer will be rung after 7 minutes.
  • Any extension in the allotted time will result in the loss of points.

  • Rules for Round 1

  • 1) Teams will be given a research paper from there area of study and they have to write the abstract and title for it.
  • 2) Selected teams will proceed to round 2.
  • 3) Based on the accuracy of title , meaningfulness of title and number of words used to write abstract and their effectiveness in explaining the research paper meaning.
  • 4) Time period- (1hr)

  • Rules for Round 2 –

  • 1) Teams will be evaluated on their communication skills and understanding of the paper given in round 1.
  • 2) 30 minutes time will be given to them to prepare the PPT.
  • 3) Every team will have to present a 5-7 min PPT to explain the paper given in round 1.
