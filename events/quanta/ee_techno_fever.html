    TECHNO FEVER

    An event which brings out the technocrat in you, where each team races against time through the daunting clue tasks to be the first in finding the hidden treasure.


  • Round 1 (Screening Round):
  • (1) Each team will be given 3 questions.
  • (2) Each member will be given a different question.
  • (3) Questions have to be solved consequently.
  • (4) First few teams will be selected for the next round.
  • Round 2 (Treasure Hunt Round):
  • (1) Teams have to search for the clues that are placed all over the B-Block.
  • (2) There will be a time limit for searching the hidden clues.
  • (3) The team with certain amount of clues will qualify for next round.
  • Round 3(final Round):
  • (1) The clues collected in the second round will be use to solve the final problem.
  • (2) The team with higher number of clues will have an upper hand to solve the final problem.
  • (3) The first two team will be declared Winner and Runner-up
