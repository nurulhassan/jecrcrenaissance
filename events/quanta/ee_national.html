    NATIONAL CONFERENCE

    If you have the propensity of scientific temper and research then this National Conference is a platform for you to show your area of interest and your contribution.


  • 1. The complete abstracts and hard copies must be submitted under IEEE format only.
  • 2. The abstracts of the papers must be sent on rtstee2019@jecrc.ac.in . Maximum Length: 1 page
  • 3. Maximum of 2 participants
  • 4. Maximum time of 8 minutes is given per team to present their papers.
  • 5. Questions will be posed by judges for 2 minutes.
  • 6. Two hard copies and one soft copy with an abstract containing 200 words are to be submitted at the time of event.
  • 7. The winners are decided on the basis of above criteria by the judges.
  • 8. No queries regarding results are entertained and the decision of the judges is final..
