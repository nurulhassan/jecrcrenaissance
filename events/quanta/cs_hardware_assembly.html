    HARDWARE ASSEMBLING

    Renaissance presents Hardware Assembling an event which is based on developing the hardware application skills of an individual. Teams will be provided with all the unassembled components of a CPU and other basic items required for assembling it. Each team will be given a maximum of 5 minutes to assemble all the parts and make it bootable (that makes it working).The team which assembles all the components correctly, make it bootable and follows all the rules and regulations in minimum time shall be declared as the winner.


  • 1.The time limit is of 5 minutes.
  • 2.The team should be of 2 members only.
  • 3.All the components should be dealt with proper care.
  • 4. Use of any unfair means will lead to straightaway disqualification.
  • 5.Teams are required to be on the time given to them.
  • 6.Teams must keep their registration slips with them
  • 7.Participants can be of any year and college.
