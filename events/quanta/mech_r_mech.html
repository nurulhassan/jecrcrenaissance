    R-MECH OLYMPIAD

    Olympiad named as ‘R-Mech’ for Mechanical Branch and for all first year Branches.

  • One student can participate.
  • Certificates would be provided to each of the participants.
  • There will be 2 rounds.
  • Round 1: There will be 40 questions for 30 minutes including our course subjects and aptitude. Marking scheme is +3 for each correct answer and -1 for each wrong answer.
  • Round 2: There will be 30 questions for 20 minutes including same course. Marking scheme is +2 and -1
  • Note :- Both the rounds will be yearwise. And the level of paper will be accordingly. And prize for all year is separate.
