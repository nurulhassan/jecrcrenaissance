    PROPELLO

    Propello is an event of making propulsion device with the use of compressed air. “PROPELLO” is the annual TECH-EVENT organised by JECRC in our techno-cultural fest RENAISSANCE.

    Design and build your own balloon-powered car and propulsion rocket that will travel as far and fast as possible.

  • 1.Maximum number of members in each team is 3.
  • 2.Material for construction will be provided on the spot.
  • 3.Participants have to construct the device within time limits.
  • 4.Judging will be done on the basis of the performance of the device.
  • 5.Propulsion rocket powered with the help of pressurised water. Judging will be done on the basis of time of flight, distance of path covered and trajectory of the device.

    Date: 16/03/2019 , Time: 10:30 AM – 1:30 PM

    Entry Fee : Rs.300 (Per Team)

    First Prize: Rs 2500/-

    Second Prize: Rs 150/-

    Venue: D-Block
