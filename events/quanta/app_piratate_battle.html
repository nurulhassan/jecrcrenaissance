    PIRATE BATTLE

    Renaissance 2k19 presents *PIRATE BATTLE* a technical event which is based on the concept of “SHIP WRECK ”.

    It is a robotic race type shipping event where the participants have to complete laps on the track in the minimum possible time opting tricky shortcuts crossing many obstacles.


  • Team should be of maximum 4 members.
  • Ships have to complete the track in minimum time.
  • Each team must declare a name for their team at the time of competition.
  • The machine you built will be ﬁrst check for size and safety. The machine should not cause electric power break downs.
  • The machine must not contain any combustible, corrosive or other dangerous material for safety reasons.
  • No explosive compression or decompression, either internal or external is permitted.
  • Judges have the right to disqualify any machine whose working mechanism or game strategy is considered hazardous in any way.
  • In case any dispute arises, the judges’ decision will be considered ﬁnal and no argument will be entertained.
  • Round 1- According to the above rule top 40% of the participants will be selected for the second round.
  • Round 2- More hurdles are added in the second round and the rule is the same
