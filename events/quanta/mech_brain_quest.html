    BRAIN QUEST

    A test of knowledge, exclusively in Mechanical Engineering discipline as a competition between Individuals and Teams.

  • Only team (maximum of 3 person) entries are eligible.
  • Maximum three teams from a college and total 10 teams will be registered for event.
  • Audiences shall not give any hints or clues to the competitors
  • Decision of judges will be final and binding.
  • Round 1- There is a small MCQ test for each team which will be of 10 minutes containing 20 questions. Questions will be on GK, Aptitude, basic mechanical engineering and some other general questions.
  • Round 2- This will be a rapid fire round. In this 5 questions will be asked to each team and for correct answer they get +2 and for wrong answer-1
  • Round 3- This will be a buzzer round. In this 10 questions will be asked and 1st team to press the buzzer will get the opportunity to answer.
