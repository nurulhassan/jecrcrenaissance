    CAD MANIA

    In this event participant are challenged to design 3-D object on AutoCAD.

  • 1.It is individual participant’s event.
  • 2.No other accessories will be provided rather than question paper
  • 3.No external electronic devices will be allowed
  • 4.If system problem occurs, it will be short out at the time
  • 5.Decision of judges will be final, cannot be argued.
  • Round 1: It is divided into two parts.
  • Quiz (15 question/15 marks/10 min.)
  • 2D-drawing (1 drawing/25 marks/30 min)
  • Round 2: The student who qualifies 1st stage will only move to the second stage.
  • Contestants will be given a 3D drawing and its all three views (F.V., T.V., and S.V.) would have to be drawn with dimensions in 90 min. (60 marks)
