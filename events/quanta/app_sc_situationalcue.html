    SITUATION CLUE

    This is an innovative event designed to test the IQ level and instant reactions of the participants to given situations. The purpose is to check the ability to think instantly and react on the spot. Candidates are expected to demonstrate verbal aptitude, confidence and a sense of humor.

  • In the first round , the participants will be given a word and from that they will have to create a sentence in 30 seconds .
  • In the second round, the winners of the first round will be given a Situation ( shown in the projector ) and they would be allotted 30 seconds to think and 60 seconds to react on it.
