  • Dance is the purest form of expression of all emotions. Some great words quote "Dance is the movement of the soul on nature's rhythm." Dancing is a pious form of art cherished both by the performer and the viewer.
  • It is a great honor for us to present enthusiastic dancers all across India with this platform of Bootstrapping'19 and have your college dance team as a part of the group dance competition. With utmost pleasure we invite your participation to make Renaissance'19 more valuable.

  • 1. Max 2 teams per college are allowed and each participant shall bring his/her valid original college ID card on the day of the event.
  • 2. The min number of participants should be 5 and maximum is 25 { on stage: max(15)}
  • 3 Pre-recorded music should be brought in a CD/DVD as well as a pen drive in .mp3 format only
  • 4.The time limit should not exceed 7+1 minute.
  • 5. The teams should bring their own portable music system for practice.
  • 6. Individuality and originality of the dance is encouraged.
  • 7. Obscenity of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • 8. Use of only stage is allowed.
  • 9. The use of fireworks & fire acts, colors, water are not allowed.
  • 10. If choreography appears to be copied, negative marks will be awarded.
  • 11. Judgment Criteria
  • Choreography
  • Expression
  • Innovation
  • Energy
  • 12. There is no restriction on the theme. All the participants in a group should belong to same technical or management institution. The decision of the judges and the organizing committee will be final.
