    THEATRE-RANGSHALA

    RENAISSANCE, the annual inter-college-techno-cultural festival of JECRC Jaipur, welcomes you to THEATRE- RANGSHALA. THEATRE, the greatest and the most interesting form of performing arts, a medium of expression of emotions and of teaching carried forward from ancient times . RENAISSANCE'19 invites you to 'THEATRE- RANGSHALA' to celebrate this art of theatre. The objective of The Theatre is to foster social and intellectual activity among the students and to create interest in and encourage talent for Drama and Theatre.
