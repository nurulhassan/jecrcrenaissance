    BEAT THE BEATS

    Music gives soul to the universe,wings to mind,flight to imagination,and life to everything.So here we bring you the best instrumental players competing with each other in a event full of fun and excitement.


  • Round I: In round one, participants will be given time for 210 seconds (3.5 min.) each to play any instrument in which they have excelled their skills and enthral the audience and judges with their melodious performance.

  • Round II: The participants selected in the 1st round will move ahead and will get a chance to make their 2nd appearance at the stage. In this round the participants will be given 300 seconds (5 min.) and they have to match their tone on the instruments with a track playing in the background.
  • Participants needs to carry their own tracks in pen drives for Round 2.
