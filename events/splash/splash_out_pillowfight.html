    PILLOW FIGHT

    This is one of the most amusing events of splash in which basically two players will fight with pillow in their hand, on 6ft. high horizontal pole. The last player to remain on pole wins.


  • 1. Players can’t use hand for attacking or defending.
  • 2. Players can’t hold the pole.
  • 3. Players can’t cross the legs for grip.
  • 4. In 2vs.2 tournament the defender can attack only attack if attacker is brought down.
  • 5. The decision of referee would be final decision.
