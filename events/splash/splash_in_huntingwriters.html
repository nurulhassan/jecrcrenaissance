    HUNTING WRITERS

    Hunting Writers, a hunt-based event full of adventures. It intrigues the participants and requires them to think with their brains to produce a surreal story and make people wonder on how did they came up with that!


  • Round I:
  • This round is about BEG BORROW STEAL, so the only rule is "HUNT FOR ALL OBJECTS", participants would be judged on basis of number of successful items hunted.

  • Round II:
  • Number of objects mentioned in the story, will be the primary judging criteria.
  • Separate points would be earned on the basis of creativity and humor and also the style of writing.
  • Participants can use only English Language.
  • Judges decision would be final.
