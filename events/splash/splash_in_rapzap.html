    RAP ZAP

    RAP ZAP is a one day event consisting of battles between the rappers to determine who is the best of em all . The event will consist of two rounds and a tie breaker if needed .

    *Winner gets the golden opportunity to record two original compositions with NUCLEAR PUKE in the recording studio*


  • The contestants should bring their own tracks in a pen drive.
  • The tracks should be strictly instrumental any other backing tracks will be disqualified.
  • Contestants are required to report 1 hour prior to the event.
  • The judging will be on the basis of stage presence, composition, flow and bars
  • For original tracks and composition extra marks will be given.

    Date:- 16/03/2019, Time 12:00 - 03:00PM

    Entry Fee : Rs 250/- (Solo)

    Entry Fee : Rs 350/- (duo/group)

    First Prize Rs 3500/-

    Second Prize Rs 2000/-

    Venue:- A Block Auditorium
