    STREET FOOTBALL

    Football is a team sport and not as individual sport, we win as a team, we lose as a team, we divide the credit and the blame. Do you have Ronaldo, Messi, and Neymar inside you??


  • Every team consists of max. 7 players (5 playing + 2 subs.).
  • There will be unlimited substitution (rolling substitution).
  • Duration of matches = a. 10-2-10 minutes playing time
  • B. 15-5-15 semi and final
  • The Goalkeeper must not hold the ball with hands in a penalty area or outside the penalty area.
  • Penalty will be taken from the center line and no goalkeeper is there and one bounce is allowed in penalty or otherwise direct.
  • There will no goal from the defending half. The goal will be considered if ball touches either of players from both teams.
  • The goal kick should not cross the center line without touching any players either of the teams.
  • There will be unlimited substitution (rolling substitution).
  • Outside will be taken by foot.
  • If match ends in a draw, the result will be decided through penalties (best of there).
  • No offside rule.
  • Time wasting: the referee can award penalty against teams which is persistently time waiting after the team has been first warned about its conduct.
  • Any misbehavior will lead to team disqualification.
