    GULLY CRICKET

    Gully Cricket is an informal game of cricket, played by people of all genders and all ages in gardens, back yards, on the street, in parks, carparks, beaches and any area not specifically intended for the purpose. Match comprises of 5-5 over from each side.


  • Every team should have 5 players.
  • Ball meets the bat means one run.
  • One tip one hand appears to be out.
  • Umpire decision is the last decision.
  • In a team only one girl can be consider.
  • For last batsman one side batting is allowed (either off side or on side as decided by player).
  • If ball touches body three times continues its means out.
  • If a player creates rift from Umpire then his team will thrown out from the championship.

  • For Girls:
  • She can hit sixes also.
  • If complete girls team when play against the boys team then their captain can hit six and their vice-captain score is multiplied by 1.5.
  • NOTE: - For a particular team, Team format as follow:-
  • (a) All boys’ members.
  • (b) All girls’ members.
  • (c) There may be 4 boys and 1 girl
