    GAME OF SERIES

    A general Quiz for the tv series nerds to test their knowledge of their favourite fandom.


  • Fandoms are FRIENDS, GAME OF THRONES, DC v/s MARVEL.

  • The event will be divided in the three rounds.

  • Round 1- The teams will choose one fandom they feel confident at and accordingly rooms will be provided. There will be a quiz related to their selected fandom, which examines expertise in their domain of interest (The fandom they choose as their forte). The teams with the most correct answers will go to the next round.

  • Round 2-This will test the puzzle solving skills of all the selected teams. It will be basically a scavenger hunt that will be aligned with your selected fandom or tv series.
