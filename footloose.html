FOOTLOOSE


" Footloose is back and this time with a bang! So lose yourself
in this three phase freestyle dance competition and let your
passion sway your body to the rhythm. Flaunt the knack of your
dance and get ready to conquer the stage with your sizzling moves.


Round-1-: Happy feet- The Showcase
● This is a showcase.
● Time will be 45 seconds.
● The dancer should bring their own 45 sec track (in cds,pendrive).
● Originality of the dance is encouraged as well as interaction & contacts with the spectators & the other dancers.
● Dancers are not restricted from performing any move but they must be aware that it is their own responsibility to ensure that they do not perform dangerous moves that could injure themselves or others.
● The use of fireworks is not allowed.

Round -2 -: Provocation- Random Song challenge
● 20 dancers will be selected from 1st round.
● There will be a random song challenge.
● Time will be 1 min 30 sec.
● Marking in this round will be done on the basis of quality of moves & synchronization with music.

Round -3 -: Bop ‘Till You Drop- Prop Mania
· 10 participants will be selected from 2nd round.
· There will be a random song prop challenge.
· Prop will be provided to the participant.
· Time will be 2 min.
· Marking in this round will be done on the basis of quality of moves & synchronization with music.
· In case of any dispute, the decision of Judges will be final.
