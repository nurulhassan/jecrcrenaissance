A lot of football success is in the mind ! You must believe you are the best and then make sure that u are . Do you have a Ronaldo, Messi or Neymar inside you?? Then for you ,the countdown begins!!

1. Teams
• Every team consists of 7 players.(5 players+2 subs.).Teams can be all male or may include max. 2 girls in team.
• The team including girls have special benefits that one penalties will be awarded to one girl player of that team.

2. Duration of matches
• 10-2-10 minutes playing time.

3. GK rules
• A GK must not score goals .
• The GK must not hold the ball longer than necessary in his penalty area (“playing for time”).
• GK should not touch the ball with hand otherwise penalties will be awarded.
• Free kick would be awarded from centre line ,only GK is allowed to stop the ball.

4. Goal scored from inside the D will not valid.

5. At least one player must remain in the opposition half of play .A foul will be awarded against a team where the whole team is in its own half.

6. Time wasting.

The refree can award penalty against team which is persistently time-wasting after the team has been first warned about its conduct.
• If match ends in a draw ,the result will be decided from penalties.
• No offside rule.
• No header allowed.
• The decision of refree will be considered final.
• Any misbehaviour will lead to team disqualification.
• All player must carry clg identity card with them.
• The team player must report 15 min prior to the match time in case of late other team will be declared as winner.
