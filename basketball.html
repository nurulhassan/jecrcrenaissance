Basketball Jam

In this event Participants will be participating in teams of minimum 3 and a maximum of 4. Basketball Jam will be a half court 3 v/s 3 match played between teams. All the rules followed will be of normal basketball games. The matches will be played with reduced quarter lengths. Fixtures and match scheduling will be done according to the entries. The Team winning after all the levels of scrutiny will be rewarded. The team standing first will be awarded Rs.1500. While the runner-up team will receive Rs.1000.

Shoot The Ball

In this event participant will shoot the basketball from various spots as instructed. A participant will be awarded as per the amount of converts from the given amount of shots offered.
