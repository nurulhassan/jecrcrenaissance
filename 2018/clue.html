SITUATIONAL CUE
  • About
  • Rules
  • Registration

This is an innovative event designed to test the instant reactions of the participants to given situations. The purpose is to check the ability to think instantly and react on the spot. Candidates are expected to demonstrate verbal aptitude, confidence and a sense of humor.

1. The situations will be flashed on the screen with the help of a projector.
2. Participants will be provided two mikes for the event.
3. The participants will be given one minute to react to the situation in the first round. After the completion of one minute the buzzer will ring
4. In the second round, the participants will be allotted 30 seconds to speak for motion and 30 seconds to speak against it.
5. No extension of time will be granted.
6. In the first round every participant would be given a situation (which is picked through chits from a basket).
7. The participants would be allotted 30 seconds to think about the situation and 60 seconds to react to it.
8. In the second round, the winners of the first round will be given a topic and they would be required to speak 30 seconds’ for’ a topic and 30 seconds ‘against’ the topic.
