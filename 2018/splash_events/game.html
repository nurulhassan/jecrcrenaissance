GAME OF SERIES

A general Quiz for the nerds to test their knowledge of their favourite fandom.
The event will be divided in the three rounds.

Round 1- The teams will choose one fandom they feel confident at and accordingly rooms will be provided. They have to answer 10-15 questions, which examines expertise in their domain of interest. The teams with the most correct answers will go to the next round.
Round 2- This will test the puzzle solving skills of all the selected teams. It will be basically a scavenger hunt. Where students have to solve different riddles and puzzles to get the next clue and the teams finishing first will go to next round.
Round 3- The final round will be a buzzer round. Here the questions will be from a wide variety of topics. Where the team first answer correctly, and score certain set points first will win.
