LAN GAMING is a technical event which provides a platform for all the students to play multiplayer computer games of different categories which makes every student bold enough to face the world.

• Counter Strike 1.6 and Counter Strike - Global Offensive
• COD 4 SinglePlayer and Multiplayer
• Tekken 5
• NFS Most Wanted
• FIFA
• DOTA 2

1. There will be a maximum 5 members team for DOTA 2.
2. No Single Student is allowed in two or more teams.
3. Multiple entries from different colleges are allowed.
4. No foul language is allowed while playing.
5. Use of any unfair means whatsoever shall result in disqualification.
6. Each team will play Captain's Mode .
