SILENT DISCO

An event at which people dance to music that is transmitted through wireless headphones,
rather than played over a speaker system.
The event ‘SILENT DISCO’ is a recreational event in which each person
will get a headphone consisting of 3-4 channels. Each person will dance according to their set channels.


1. Each person will get a slot of 1 hour.
2. There are 5 slots on event day-:
a.) 10am - 11am
b.) 11:15am - 12:15pm
c.) 12:30pm - 1:30pm
d.) 1:45pm - 2:45pm
e.) 3:00pm - 4:00pm
