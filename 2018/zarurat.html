ZARURAT

Zarurat - the help beyond is a social endeavor by the JECRC foundation which focuses on imparting
a healthy all round development of the underprivileged children from the nearby slum areas.
Keeping up with the spirit of Renaissance,
the pre-Renaissance event “Zarurat - celebrating innocence” is celebrated,
seeing a footfall of 350 kids from various NGOs and 200 student volunteers.

Continuing the spirit of celebration, Zarurat will be organising this year's event on 24th March 2018.


ZUMBA DANCE : Zumba Dance is an aerobic exercise which is kept as an inaugural activity to give an energetic start to the full day event.

PRASTUTI : A platform for the kids to showcase their talent.

KALAKRITI : A platform for the children to go beyond their horizons and create a world of their imagination.

HUNGAMA : A magic show is organized for the children.

AWARENESS SESSION : A session organized for kids where they will be made aware of the Swachch Bharat campaign will be presented by TEAM SOCH.

BAL MAHOTSAV : A kids carnival for the fun and entertainment of the kids.
