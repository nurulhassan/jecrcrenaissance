Cut2design has the main motto to understand the Machine Drawing.
In this regard, we have enclosed this event with 2 rounds.
First round is for checking the theoretical knowledge about Machine drawing basics.
Second round about making three dimensional object of given 2D view.

Team of 2 members participation.

Round 1-: Theoretical quiz consisting 30 questions in 20 minutes
Round 2-: 2D view consisting elevation , plan and side view
By the help of this drawing students have to make 3d Object by Soap.

Guidelines-: For the participation Soap and tools should be provided.
