Antakshri also known as Anthyakshari is a spoken parlor game played in India.Each contestant sings the first verse of a song (usually a Bollywood song) that begins with the Hindustani consonant on which the previous contestant's song selection ended.


ROUND 1:-
SHABD JAAL- SEVERAL WORDS WILL BE WRITTEN IN CHITS AND THEN IT WILL BE PLACED IN A BOWL.EACH TEAM MEMBER WILL HAVE TO COME TAKE OUT THE CHIT AND WHICHEVER WORD THEY GET, THEY HAVE TO SING A SONG WITH THAT WORD IN IT. TIME LIMIT TO SING THE SONG: 15-20 SEC.

ROUND 2:-
DUMB CHARADES: - EACH MEMBER OF THE TEAM WILL BE GIVEN A NAME OF A MOVIE.HIS/HER TEAM MEMBERS HAVE TO DEPICT THE NAME OF THAT MOVIE AND SING ANY SONG OF THAT MOVIE.TOTAL TWO NAMES WILL BE GIVEN TO EACH TEAM.

ROUND 3:-
PEHCHAAN KON:-EACH TEAM WILL BE GIVEN NAMES OF ACTOR AND ACTRESS AND THEY WILL HAVE TO SING A SONG FEATURING THE GIVEN ACTOR AND ACTRESS.TOTAL 4 PER TEAM.

ROUND 4:-
BUZZER BAJAO:-IN THIS ROUND THE LAST 2 TEAMS WILL BE THERE.WE WILL PLAY SEVERAL SONG, THE FIRST TEAM TO HIT THE BUZZER WILL TELL THE NAME OF THE MOVIE AND POINTS WILL BE GIVEN ACCORDINGLY,TOTAL 11 SONGS WILL BE PLAYED.
