ELECTRO QUIZZERS

A Quiz event

1. In a team the maximum of 3 members.
2. Quiz will be based on the basic electrical and electronics syllabus with some interesting questions.
3. There will be 3 rounds.
4. Teams not having cut off marks in each round will be eliminated.
5. In case of any tie there will be sudden death question where team giving correct answer will qualify for next round.
6. 1st round will be visual round where questions will be displayed and students will have to answer them.
7. 2nd round will be based on general and logical technical questions.
8. 3rd and final round will be buzzer round.
9. Team with maximum points after the end of 3th round will be declared winner of the quiz.
10. In case of any conflict the decision of judges would be final.
11. Use of internet will be strictly prohibited. Any team found with this would be disqualified.

Participation fee-: Rs. 150/- (Each Team)
