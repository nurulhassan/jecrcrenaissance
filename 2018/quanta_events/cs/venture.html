NEW VENTURE

BUSINESS PLAN COMPETITION
“NEW VENTURE” is the annual Business Plan competition organized by JECRC in our Techno-Cultural Fest *Renaissance*
The event seeks to identify budding entrepreneurs that may have the potential to contribute
to the buzzing economy in the coming future.

1. Participants are required to register and submit B- Plan abstract online in word or Pdf format with the name, New Venture_Business Plan
2. Abstract Length- Maximum of 1 pages.
3. The abstract should contain the name of the business, team members and the institution.
4. Abstract submission will be started from 10th March 2018 and it should be mailed at edc@jecrc.ac.in. Last date of abstract submission will be on or before 22nd March 2018 (11:59 PM).
New Venture_Business Plan
5. The subject line of the e-mail should read as
New Venture_Business Plan
6. A team can have one or more members with a maximum of 4 in a team.
7. Submit your business plan as soon as possible for more chances of selection in Phase 1.
Decisions of the judges would be final.

Registration Fees -: Rs. 500(Per Team)
1st Prize: Cash Reward of ₹ 11000
10 Consolation prizes
