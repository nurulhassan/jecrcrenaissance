KALAKRITI

Kalakrti is basically about the crafting and painting skills of a team where a team has
to use the given waste material to give it an innovative look. A team has to use compulsorily
at least 2 materials given in the packet and one more item provided to each team.
After the event , the team has to clean the table and keep the craft item on it and the
waste and left out item again into the packet provided.

1.Time limit will be 2 hours.
2. Team should be of 3 members.
3. Use of 2 material and 1 special item is compulsory.
4. Materials would be provided to each team in the starting of event in fixed amount, NO extra mater7. Marks would be given on the basis of best use of materials provided, creativity and cleanlinessial will be provided.
5. Exchange of material from other teams is strictly not allowed.
6. Marks would be given on the basis of best use of materials provided, creativity and cleanliness
7. Use of mobile phones is strictly prohibited.
8. Team have to bring their own colours, geometry box, scissors and fevicol etc.
9. Team have to keep the table clean . As points for cleanliness will be counted.
10. Student of any year can participate in this event.

FEES: Rs.150 (Per Team)
1st Prize: Rs2000
2nd Prize: Rs1000
