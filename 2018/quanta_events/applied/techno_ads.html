TECHNO-ADS

JECRC TechnoAds is basically a techincal event in which a team has to choose one
product out of 5 products kept on the table and design a logo for the product chosen.
Teams whose logo will be selected after scrutiny are allowed to participate in next round.
in second round , the team has to make punch line for the product and prepare a jingle for that product.
Team has to perform jingle on the stage within time limit of 3 minutes.

1. Team should be of 2-3 members.
2. Use of internet connection is prohibited. 3. In first round only 45 minutes will be given for logo formation and the team who cross time limit will be disqualified.
4. After scrutiny only 12 team will go for second round of jingle performance.
5. Only 3 minutes will be given to per team for jingle proformance.
6. The team with best logo and best jingle performance will be awarded.
7. The teams have to use photoshop software for logo designing.
8. Any extension in the allotted time will result in the disqualification of team
9. Use of phone is prohibited.
10. Teams will be judged on the basis of their creativity and imagination.
11. Final decision will lie with the Judges.

FEES: Rs.150(Per Team)
1st Prize: Rs1000
2nd Prize: Rs700
