Let Me Speak

Each participant will be given three minutes to speak extempore on a topic picked up by him or her.
A bunch of the topics will be given to select any one randomly.
He/she will be given one minute prior to the speech to think over the topic.
Extempore is a public speech delivered without preparation.

1) The participant has to speak only in English.
2) Maximum time limit for thinking over the topic will be 1 minute and maximum time for speech will be 3 minutes
3) The participant will be allowed to select the topic maximum twice. In case he or she decides to select the topic second time, then he or she will not be allowed to take up the first topic.
4) Participant will not be allowed to take any paper or material with him or her during the speech
5) Each Participant shall be given two minutes to speak on a topic picked up by him/her by draw of lots.
6) A bell will ring at the end of one minute to indicate that one minute is left to finish his/her speech.
7) Another bell will ring at the end of two minutes.
8) Marks will be deducted if the speaker exceeds the time limit.
9) Language used shall be strictly English.
10) No objection shall be entertained as the Decision of the Judges shall be final.

FEES-: Rs. 100/- (per participant)

First prize – Rs. 2000/-,
Second prize – Rs. 1000/-,
Third prize – Rs. 500/-
