R-Mech Olympiad

A real test of engineering aptitude for the Mechanical Engineering students.
Round 1:
1. 50 Basic Mechanical Engineering questions are to be answered in 60 minutes.
2. 50% students will be qualified for the next round.
3. The qualified students will be further divided as per their year of study.
Round 2:
1. 30 core Mechanical Engineering questions are to be answered in 30 minutes.
2. Participation certificates will be given to every student who qualified for the 2nd round.

1. This event is for JECRC students only.
2. The participants shall not be allowed to use mobile or any other electronics instruments except scientific calculator.
3. During the exam students are not allowed to leave the room for drinking water and any other services.

Entry Fee : Rs.100 (Per Participant)
1st Prize : Rs.1500 (Each Year)
2nd Prize : Rs.750 (Each Year)
Consolation Prize : Rs. 300 (2 in Each Year)
