HUNTING WRITERS
  • About

Hunt and then write, thus the name of the event goes by “Hunting Writers”.

There will be 2 rounds & the participants can take part as a pair.

In the 1st round participants have to go on a hunt, to find a list of objects, provided by the volunteers in a given time interval. The number of objects and the points collected will decide their qualification to the second round.

In the 2nd round the qualified team has to write a story using the objects in their list and the winner will be decided depending upon how exciting & meaningful their story is.

Rules & Regulation will be disclosed at the time of Event.
