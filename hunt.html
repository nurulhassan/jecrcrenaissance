TREASURE HUNT
  • About

  • Registration

Treasure hunt is the type of event by which participants check there knowledge in a creative way. It also develops the problem solving quality of the student and also check the presence of mind during solving of problems. By solving different question participants get clues to reach at a particular place and carry on the event.

Rules :

• Electronics items are not allowed.
• There is time limit to solve the question.

Guidelines :

1. There will be a team of maximum 3 members.
2. Single participant or member of two teams are allowed but they need to pay required registration fees.
3. Event will be organized in slots i.e. particular number of teams are playing in different slots.
4. The participants will be given 15-20 minutes to complete the slot i.e. to solve all questions.
5. If team will not present on time given by coordinators they will be disqualify at a time i.e. team should be present on time at given venue.

Judgement Criteria :

There will be two rounds: In 1st round there are different slots in which 5 teams at a time participate and there will 6 slots and each winning team of each slot will go for 2nd round which is final round and in second round, winner will be decided.
