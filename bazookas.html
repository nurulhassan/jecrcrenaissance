• The complete abstracts and hard copies must be submitted under IEEE format only.
• The abstracts of the papers must be sent to paper.ee@gmail.com
• Maximum of 2 participants.
• Maximum time of 8 minutes is given per team to present their papers.
• Questions will be posed by judges for 2 minutes.
• Two hard copies and one soft copy with an abstract containing 200 words are to be submitted at the time of event.
• The winners are decided on the basis of above criteria by the judges.
• No queries regarding results are entertained and the decision of the judges is final.

• 1st round will be visual round where scientific principal or mathematical relation will be displayed and students will have to answer about the scientist who proposed that theory
• 2nd round will be theme based questions (Buzzer round),in this round themes will be already given to participants and a picture displayed on basis of that theme, participant have to tell what is that and where that is situated.
• 3rd and final round will be based GIF that will display on screen with assembling the part of an machine final, candidates have to recognize the parts, who will ans. Maximum part’s name that will be winner .
• In case of any conflict the decision of judges would be final.
• Use of internet will be strictly prohibited. Any team found with this would be disqualified.
