Brain Quest

ROUND 1:
 Stage-I: This round is paper round of 10 min. in which each team would be given a set of 20 MCQ,
Stage-II -: Each team will be asked 10 questions one after another in five minute time.
ROUND 2:
 Stage-I-: • A question will be asked to a team and if they are unable to answer it will be passed to the next team.
Answering time is only 15 seconds.
Stage-II -: Identify the object with the help of gesture.
Stage-III -: • 10 questions would be asked. The team who presses the buzzer first will answer

1. Only team (maximum of 3 person) entries are eligible.
2. Maximum three teams from a college and total 10 teams will be registered for event.
3. Audiences shall not give any hints or clues to the competitors.
4. Decision of judges will be final and binding.
