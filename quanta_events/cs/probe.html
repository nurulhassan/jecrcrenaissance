TECH PROBE

Victory is just a few taps away, all you need is a smartphone and your
repository of technical knowledge. Tech Probe is a tech quiz with a twist.
Instead of conventional method, participants answer timed questions through their smartphones.
Grab the opportunity and showcase your talent.

1. Only team entries are eligible.
2. Team shall contain 2 members only.
3. The decision of the quiz-master will be final and will not be subjected to any change.
4. The participants shall not be allowed to use mobile or other electronic instruments.
5. The questions will be in the form of MCQ’s or specific answer.
6. Audience shall not give any clue to the competitors.
7. Teams can’t be modified in any case.
8. A question will be fired & then the team who presses the buzzer first will get to answer it within 5 seconds.
9. In case of tie between 2 or more teams, further 5 questions would be asked for final selection.

Entry Fees is Rs. 200.
Grand Prize of Rs. 5000.
