EMBRYO

Embryo is a national level paper presentation contest which provides a mode to
enhance the doors to explore different Scientific & Technical researches.
It ignites innovative ideas and enhances the stage skills of individual giving
them an opportunity to think and helps in bringing out professionalism.
So, gather your research, proliferate your energy and gear up for EMBRYO-2K18 with full enthusiasm.

1. First stage involves submitting paper in IEEE format at embryo2k18@gmail.com. Shortlisted entries will be called to present their paper in RENAISSANCE’18 at JECRC campus on 25th March 2018. The length of paper should not exceed 4 pages
. 2. Maximum 2 students per team are allowed to present a paper. Both members have to be present at event.
3. Presentation should be in .ppt or .pdf format. Contestants need to bring 2 hard copies of their submitted paper and a soft copy of their presentation in a pendrive.
4. Time for one presentation will be 5-7 minute, one buzzer will rung after 5 minute to remind of time and participants have to conclude the presentation in a total of 7 minutes.
5. The papers will be judged on the basis of their innovation, in depth knowledge of the context and presentation skills.
6. Any extension in the allotted time will result in the loss of points.
7. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Fees: Entry Fee : Rs. 200 (per team)
1st Prize : Rs. 2100
10 Consolation Prizes : Rs. 500 Each
