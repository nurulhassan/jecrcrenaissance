ZENITH

The event here explores the creativity and understanding of the structure.
In this event, participants have to construct a structure of roof and floor
standing more or less permanently in one place provided props


1. The participating team must not comprise of more than 3 persons.
2. Use of props other than provided is prohibited.
3. Use of supports is however allowed.
4. Newspapers and Thermocols will be provided by the organizers to the participating team.
5. Participants must carry their own pair of ruler, scissors or any other cutting tool.

Entry Fees: INR 250

Prize: 1st prize- INR 2000/-
2nd prize- INR 1500/-
